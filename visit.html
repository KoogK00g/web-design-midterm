Visit Webster

Our St. Louis, Missouri campus is in Webster Groves, a tree-lined community developed in the late 19th century, featuring an abundance of cultural offerings, small business-owned restaurants and boutiques. It is just 15 minutes away from downtown St. Louis, where our students have endless entertainment and education-related opportunities. Visiting campus is potentially the most important step in deciding which university you will attend. Here you can begin to experience the uniqueness of Webster. Students often remark that their campus visit is one of the most important factors in deciding to attend Webster University. Join us on a tour of our beautiful, 47-acre campus and get your questions answered. Learn more about the region and all that it has to offer. If you cannot make it to campus, we also offer a number of virtual visit experiences. In person or online ... we can't wait to see you!
